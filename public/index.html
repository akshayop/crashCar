About this Game

Crash car- An extreme car driving tour is a mile stone in the journey of endless arcade racing. Drive your car on the highway and earn points.Try to be one of the fastest drivers.

How To Play

The player needs to move along the road,overtaking other cars and avoiding a collision.The distance traveled and the collected points increase the player's score.

Controls

A player can move the car left and right by clicking left and right arrow keys.
